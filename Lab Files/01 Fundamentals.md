# FAQ

* What is AI? (Artificial Intelligence)[^1]
	* Artificial intelligence, or AI, is software that is designed to perform tasks that typically require human thinking, such as understanding language, recognizing patterns, and making decisions. It works by processing large amounts of data to find patterns and use those patterns to generate responses or take actions. AI does not think or reason the way humans do; it is a sophisticated pattern-matching system trained to produce useful outputs.
* How do chatbots like ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, and Perplexity fit in the AI world?[^2]
	* These chatbots are a category of AI called large language models, or LLMs, which are trained on massive amounts of text data to understand and generate human language. They are designed to hold conversations, answer questions, summarize information, help with writing, and assist with a wide range of tasks. The interface is a simple chat, embedded in a web page or window for commercial users. LLMs can also be accessed via command line interface (CLI), stand-alone GUI app, or added to other apps via plugins.[^3]

## Friendly Suggestions ;-) 

If you want to go deep down the rabbit hole that is artificial intelligence, I recommend that you do the following things in the following order:

1. Take or brush up on fundamental math skills
2. Take statistics
3. Take a traditional data science course

Do those three things and you will have a better, more thorough understanding of both data science and artificial intelligence then you can get from a full on computer degree. That's the heart of it. Once you have achieved those three things, you will know enough to ask the questions to take you further down the path you need to take. If you are going pursue programming of any kind, use these prompts to create your own tutor so that you can better learn how programming languages work and programming in general work.
